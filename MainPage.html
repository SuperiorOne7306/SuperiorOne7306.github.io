Oculus Quest

Introduction

The Oculus Quest is a wireless VR headset system designed by Oculus. It's the 2nd fully wireless headset that Oculus have made, the first being Oculus Go. With a price of $550CN - $700CN, it's one of the most easily accesible VR headsets out there, mainly because the headset doesn't need an expensive gaming computer to run, which can cost an extra $1000 or even more.

The point of this website is to inform the viewers about the Oculus Quest, as well as tell you my opinons on the Quest.
